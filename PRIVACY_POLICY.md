# Privacy Policy for BoB Bot

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to BoB Bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use BoB Bot on Discord.

By using BoB Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use BoB Bot, we automatically collect the following information:

- **Discord User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier (snowflake ID)
- **Discord Guild/Server ID**: The unique identifier of servers where the bot is used
- **Username**: Your Discord username for display purposes
- **Avatar URL**: Your Discord profile picture URL for display in bot embeds

### User-Generated Data

When you interact with the bot, we collect and store:

- **Economy Data**: Balance, bank amount, cookies, tickets, and transaction history
- **Experience & Levels**: XP points, current level, and progression statistics
- **Inventory Data**: Collected animals, weapons, items, and equipment
- **Activity Statistics**: 
  - Total voice minutes in voice channels
  - Command usage frequency (count only)
  - Daily/weekly reward claim timestamps
  - Work command cooldowns
- **Social Data**:
  - Marriage partner information (if you use the marriage feature)
  - Reputation points given/received
  - Custom nicknames for animals/items
- **Server Settings**: 
  - Custom prefix preferences per server (set by administrators)
  - Welcome/goodbye message configurations

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

- ‚ùå Message content (except command invocations for immediate processing)
- ‚ùå Private conversations or DMs (unless you directly command the bot)
- ‚ùå Email addresses
- ‚ùå Real names or personal identifiers
- ‚ùå IP addresses

- ‚ùå Voice recordings
- ‚ùå Images or files you send
- ‚ùå Presence/status information (beyond temporary caching)

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve bot functionality:

   - Track progression (levels, experience, achievements)

   - Execute game mechanics and battles

2. **Personalization**
   - Display your username and avatar in bot responses
   - Remember your preferences and settings
   - Show your statistics and achievements

3. **Server Features**
   - Welcome new members with automated messages
   - Track server-specific statistics
   - Generate server leaderboards

4. **Analytics & Improvement**
   - Monitor command usage to identify popular features
   - Detect and fix bugs
   - Improve bot performance and reliability

5. **Fraud Prevention**
   - Prevent currency farming and abuse
   - Enforce cooldowns and rate limits
   - Detect automated/bot accounts
